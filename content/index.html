Hey there

My name is Patrick Brosset. I'm a senior product manager on the Edge team at Microsoft, where I work on a range of technologies such as the Web platform, Progressive Web Apps, and DevTools. Previously, I worked at Mozilla, on the Firefox DevTools team.
I'm also on the governing committee of Open Web Docs and one of the co-chairs of the W3C WebDX community group.

I have about twenty years of experience with web technologies, and have been working on browsers for the past ten years.

On this website, I like writing and talking about the web, and what it can do for developers. I also sometimes create little experiments with web technologies.
