Constraints fueled creativity

  • Heavily constrained by the platform
  • No responsive design yet
  • We made things happen with limited tools
  • Hacking with tables and sliced images
  • Playing pushed the platform forward
  • That's still how things work today

We were pretty heavily constrained by the capabilities of the platform at the time. We didn't have all the tools that we now have.

But we made things happen anyway, with a limited amount of tools. And these constraints kind of fueled our creativity.

We were hacking with tables and sliced images, not because it was elegant, but because we were playing with the only tools we had.

All this playing and attempting to do things that weren't possible, was how we pushed the platform forward. And that's still how things work today.
