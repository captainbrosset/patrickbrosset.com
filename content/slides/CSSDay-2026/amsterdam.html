Inspiration

Maximalist, old-world, canal-house grandma... except your grandma is secretly a glamorous aristocrat with a glitter addiction.

Biking much?

Park your bike, come back two minutes later, and it's now a 400-bike abstract sculpture. Is this one even mine? Looking for more? Just go fishing in the canals.

Fountain diplomacy

The statue was giving strong administrative energy, like it had just approved a permit for one very dramatic splash and then immediately needed a lunch break.

Carbonated loneliness

That bottle has the exact posture of someone waiting for a tram that only exists in the imagination of a pigeon.

Sky paperwork

Those lines overhead are clearly a memo from the clouds, written in a language that only damp bread can fully understand.

Underpass philosophy

Being under a bridge is just nature's way of saying, please pause here and reconsider your entire relationship with geometry.

Vehicle migration

There are no cars because they have all gone to a secret meeting about how to look important while standing still.

Evening calibration

The sun here does not set so much as file a final report, nod politely, and exit through the most photogenic door available.

Church of photons

That light is doing cathedral work. It arrived early, adjusted the mood, and then refused to explain its methods.
