.layout {
  display: grid;
  display: grid-lanes;
}
Grid-lanes is good at occupying empty space. But the experience you're designing might not revolve entirely around this. And having a slightly less space-efficient alternative may be fine. If so, then grid-lanes can be a great progressive enhancement. Define both display:grid and display:grid-lanes, and non supporting browsers will ignore the second.
