About

Patrick is a multidisciplinary artist based in Amsterdam, working across photography, painting, and digital media. His work draws on architecture, memory, and the textures of everyday life to create images that feel both familiar and otherworldly.

With exhibitions in over 15 countries and collaborations with institutions like MoMA, the Tate, and the Centre Pompidou, Patrick's practice continues to push the boundaries between fine art and documentary storytelling.

When not in the studio, he leads workshops on visual composition and mentors emerging artists through the European Photography Collective.
