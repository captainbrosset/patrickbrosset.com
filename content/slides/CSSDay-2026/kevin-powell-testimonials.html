--- layout: slide.njk title: Fun with grid-lanes - CSSDay 2026 - Testimonials from Kevin Powell's CSS course notes: | Testimonials are a great use case for grid-lanes. These ones here are from Kevin Powell's CSS course website. Which, by the way, you should totally check out if you haven't. Kevin implemented this with multi-column, which is great, and perfectly valid. There's no one right answer, here the order of content isn't super important. Grid-lanes can do this very easily too, but the order's going to be different than multicol. Again, multicolumn fills columns entirely before moving to the next, whereas grid-lanes fills them one by one. The implication here is that you might be pulling these quotes from a database, and you might want to always see the latest ones first, near the top. Grid-lanes would give you this for free. ---
