I am a fancy box!
Before we do, let's take a moment to appreciate where we come from. You see, to create any part of these sites at the time, we needed a lot of images. Even just for a fancy box like this, we'd have to create a div with 9 children elements, and make custom images for each of the corners and sides, repeating them as needed, to create the illusion of a single box.
