About Me

Hey! I'm Patrick. I'm a web designer and I absolutely LOVE the web! There's nothing cooler than creating stuff on the internet and sharing it with people all over the world. I spend most of my time learning HTML and Flash. The web is the future!





That's me!



My web design tools
Microsoft FrontPage FrontPage is the best! It has all these cool templates and wizards that make it super easy to create awesome websites. I don't even need to know HTML to make a great site!
Macromedia Dreamweaver Dreamweaver is amazing when I want more control. It lets me see the code AND the design view at the same time. It's like magic for HTML and tables!
Adobe Photoshop 6 Photoshop is where I make all my buttons, logos, and backgrounds. Layer effects are so cool and my graphics look super pro now.
Macromedia Fireworks Fireworks is perfect for slicing up images for the web. It makes rollovers and button states really easy, and the export looks great.
Microsoft Paint Paint is simple but awesome for quick edits. I use it when I just need to crop something fast or make a tiny GIF.
Notepad Sometimes I go hardcore and edit HTML by hand in Notepad. It feels super geeky and I learn a lot about how pages really work.
