Example: XHR → Fetch

  • XMLHTTP: IE5 ActiveX experiment (2000), Outlook team
  • Used in weird ways → GMail, Web2.0, the AJAX era
  • Reverse-engineered by other browsers
  • Standardized as XMLHttpRequest
  • Much later: informed fetch()

The web improves because developers try new things, experiment, and complain loudly enough. Then, later, specs happen.
